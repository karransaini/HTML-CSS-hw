Latitude vs. The Weather

In this assignment, we tracked how various weather patterns change geographically by latitude. This allows us to compare measurements near and far from the equator. The weather data points are taken from OpenWeatherMap API and includes 500+ cities in the data.

We analyzed the following aspects of the weather: temperature, cloudiness, humidity, and windspeed.
